Data Exploration

Hierarchical Clustering

Users can view dendrograms of total RNA-Seq, miRNA-Seq, and circRNA-Seq samples across 11 tissues. The data were normalized using DESeq2, log2 transformed, and clustered using Ward's method.


Expression Profile

Users can enter a linear RNA, miRNA, or circRNA to visualize barplots of mean expression for each tissue. For log2 transformed data, we take the log of the mean expression. When the mean expression is zero, we keep it at zero on the log2 scale.


Correlation Analysis

Users can visualize scatterplots between pairs of entered linear RNAs, miRNAs, and circRNAs. The Pearson and Spearman correlation values are displayed on top of the scatterplot.
